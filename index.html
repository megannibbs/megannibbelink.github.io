Megan Nibbelink, MSc


PhD Candidate @ the University of Kansas

Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology

Biodiversity Institute Phylogenetic Modeling Division

My research interests include statistical & computational phylogenetics, plant morphology & anatomy, and paleobotany as they relate to living and fossil ferns and lycophytes.

Aside from my research, I am a passionate advocate for equity and access in education and promoting the presence of women and minorities in STEM fields.
