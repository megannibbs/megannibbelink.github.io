Megan Nibbelink, MSc




PhD student @ the University of Kansas

Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology

Biodiversity Institute Paleobotanical Division



My first publication in the upcoming International Journal of Plant Sciences special issue in honor of Francis Heuber has been published ahead of print. You can access the PDF here.


My research interests include paleobotany, plant morphology & anatomy, systematic theory, and computational biology as they relate to living and fossil ferns and lycophytes.

Aside from my research, I am a passionate advocate for equity and access in education and promoting the presence of women and minorities in STEM.
