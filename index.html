Megan Nibbelink, MSc.

PhD student @ the University of Kansas

Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology
Biodiversity Institute Paleobotanical Division

My research interests include paleobotany, plant morphology & anatomy, systematic theory, and computational biology as they relate to living and extinct ferns and lycophytes.

Aside from my research, I am a passionate advocate for equity and access in education. I am excited to begin working with KU's Center for Digital Inclusion soon on a project that aims to help women who have been involved with the justice system build computer literacy skills.
