Hi there, I'm Megan Nibbelink!

I'm a PhD student in the Ecology and Evolutionary Biology Department at the University of Kansas. My research interests include paleobotany, plant morphology & anatomy, systematic theory, and computational biology as they relate to living and extinct ferns and lycophytes. Aside from my research, I am an avid supporter of equity and accessibility in education. I have recently begin working with KU's Center for Digital Inclusion on a project to help women who have been involved with the justice system build computer literacy skills.
